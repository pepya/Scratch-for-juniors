# 🐱 ScratchJr Adventure — Free Coding Curriculum for Kids

> A complete 6-week home learning program for kids aged 7–10. Daily 1-hour lessons, printable activity cards, teacher scripts, and everything you need — even if you've never coded before.

🌐 **Live site:** [https://pepya.github.io/scratch-for-juniors](https://pepya.github.io/scratch-for-juniors)

## 🖨️ Printing the Activity Cards

1. Open `pages/activity-cards.html` in your browser
2. Press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac)
3. Set **Layout** to Portrait, **Margins** to Minimum
4. Each card prints on its own page
5. Cut and place one card next to the tablet each day

## 📱 What You Need

- **ScratchJr app** — Free on [App Store (iPad)](https://apps.apple.com/app/scratchjr/id895485086) and [Google Play (Android)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.scratchjr.android)

## 📚 What Kids Learn

| Week | Theme | Key Concepts |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Hello, ScratchJr! | Motion blocks, triggers, looks blocks |
| 2 | Tell a Story | Pages, dialogue, timing with wait blocks |
| 3 | Loops & Repeats | Repeat loops, forever loops, patterns |
| 4 | Messages | Send/receive messages, event-driven logic |
| 5 | Sound & Music | Sound effects, voice recording, synchronisation |
| 6 | Grand Finale | Independent design, full project, presentation |

## 💡 Teaching Philosophy

This curriculum is built on three principles:

1. **Ask, don't tell** — Every lesson includes guiding questions. Children learn more by discovering than being shown.
2. **Bugs = learning** — When something breaks, celebrate it. "Something unexpected happened — let's be detectives!"
3. **Art first** — Encouraging kids to draw their own characters and worlds increases investment and motivation dramatically.
