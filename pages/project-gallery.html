Space Adventure!

A rocket launches into space and meets a friendly alien. The alien has never seen a human before — what do they say to each other? Draw a space background, a rocket, and your alien.

2
Draw a rocket (or use the emoji) — make it move up across the screen
3
Draw a funny alien — make it say "Hello Earthling!" when the flag is pressed

Week 1 😄 Comedy ⭐ Starter
The Great Chase!

A dog spots a cat and starts chasing it across the garden! The cat runs one way, the dog runs the other. They both end up running in circles. Who will catch who?

🔵 Move left/right 🟡 Tap trigger 🟣 Say
Quick start steps
1
Draw a garden background — green grass, maybe a fence
2
Add a dog character — when tapped, it moves right with "WOOF!"
3
Add a cat — when green flag pressed, it runs left saying "MEOW!"
I'll save you!

Week 1 💪 Action ⭐ Starter
My Superhero!

Draw YOUR OWN superhero — a totally original one with a name you invent. What's their superpower? They fly across the city, grow huge, and announce their arrival. The world is saved!

🔵 Move 🟣 Grow big 🟣 Say 🟡 Green flag
Quick start steps
1
Draw your superhero — cape, mask, logo — make them unique!
2
On green flag: fly right, grow BIG, say their superhero name
3
When tapped: they say "The city is safe!" and shrink back down

The Haunted House

A brave explorer enters a spooky house on a dark night. Inside: a friendly ghost who just wants someone to talk to! Three scenes — outside the house, inside the dark hallway, and the ghost's hidden room.

📄 Pages (3) 💬 Say blocks ⏳ Wait 🟣 Hide/Show
Quick start steps
1
Page 1: dark night outside a spooky house — character walks in
2
Page 2: dark hallway — ghost appears from the shadows (hide → show!)
3
Page 3: ghost's cosy room — they become friends and have tea 🍵

Week 2 🌊 Story ⭐⭐ Intermediate
Deep Ocean Friends

A little fish gets lost in the big ocean and asks different sea creatures for directions home. An octopus, a crab, and a whale all give different advice. Which way is home?

📄 Pages (3) 💬 Dialogue ⏳ Wait timing 🎨 Draw background

1
Draw a blue ocean background with coral and seaweed
2
Each page: little fish meets a new creature — 4-line conversation with wait blocks
3
Final page: fish finds home — big celebration!

My Cooking Show!

You are the host of a cooking show! Each page is a step in making your favourite food. Narrate everything with speech bubbles. The audience is watching — make it dramatic!

3
Page 4: the finished dish appears — chef grows BIG and takes a bow!

Candy Land Dance Party!

Everything in Candy Land comes alive and dances — lollipops spin, gummies bounce, cupcakes wobble. Five characters, five different forever loops, all dancing at the same time to an invisible beat.

Week 3 🔭 Science ⭐⭐⭐ Challenge
My Solar System!

Build a working solar system where every planet orbits at a different speed! The sun stays still, Mercury spins fast, Saturn spins slowly. Each planet has a forever loop — but with different timing.

♾️ Forever loops 🔵 Turn + Move ⏳ Wait (timing)
Quick start steps
1
Draw a black space background — add a bright sun in the middle
2
Draw 4 planets of different sizes — position them at different distances
3
Each planet: forever loop → turn + move. Different turn numbers = different speeds!

Week 4 🏭 Game/Story ⭐⭐ Intermediate
Robot Factory!

Build an assembly line! Robot A moves along and sends a message to Robot B → B adds a part and sends to Robot C → C finishes the robot and sends a WIN message → confetti explodes everywhere!

📨 Messages (chain) 🔵 Move 🟣 Show/Hide ⏳ Wait
Quick start steps
1
Draw a factory background with conveyor belt sections
2
3 robot characters — each receives a message, does something, sends the next message
3
Final robot: receives DONE → grows big → says "COMPLETE!" → confetti appears!
Expecto! ✨

Week 4 ✨ Fantasy ⭐⭐⭐ Challenge
Wizard Duel!

Two wizards face off! Wizard A casts a spell → sends a ZAP message → Wizard B reacts (grows huge, wobbles, says "My turn!") → B sends back a COUNTER spell → A flies backwards and lands on their hat!

📨 Messages (2-way) 👆 Tap trigger 🟣 Grow/Shrink 💬 Say
Quick start steps
1
Draw a dramatic castle or forest background
2
Wizard A: tap → ZAP message → sparkle effect; Wizard B: receives ZAP → reacts dramatically
3
Wizard B then sends COUNTER → Wizard A reacts and ends the duel with a funny bow!

My Weather Show!

You are the TV weather forecaster! Record your voice narrating today's weather AND tomorrow's. Sun appears, then clouds, then rain — each one timed with your voice narration perfectly.

3
Add background music sounds — chirping birds for sunny, rain taps for rainy!

Week 5 🎶 Music Video ⭐⭐⭐ Challenge
Lullaby for a Dragon 🐲

A tiny dragon can't fall asleep. Sing it a lullaby — record YOUR VOICE singing! As the song plays, stars appear one by one, the dragon slowly shrinks (getting sleepy), and finally falls asleep surrounded by moonlight.

🎤 Singing voice ⏳ Precise timing 🟣 Grow/Shrink 🟣 Show (stars)
Quick start steps
1
Draw a night sky — dark blue, with hidden stars (start hidden!)
2
Record yourself singing a lullaby — any melody, even made up!
3
Time the stars appearing and dragon shrinking to match your song beats

My Dream Garden!

The Time Machine!

You've built a time machine! Page 1: press a button → it vibrates and flashes → jumps to Page 2 (DINOSAURS!) → tap a dinosaur → it sends you back to Page 1 → press the button again → jump to Page 3 (FUTURE with robots!). Two different time periods to explore!

📄 Pages (3) 👆 Tap triggers 📨 Messages 🔊 Sounds 🟣 Flash effects
Quick start steps
1
Page 1: time machine control panel — draw all the buttons and levers!
2
Tap the big red button → machine vibrates (grow/shrink fast) → jumps to Page 2
3
Each time period has its own characters, sounds, and a way to travel back!

Week 6 🐾 Story ⭐⭐ Intermediate
A Day in My Pet's Life!

Tell the story of a whole day from your pet's point of view — or an imaginary pet if you don't have one! Morning wake-up, walk in the park, lunchtime, afternoon nap with zzz's floating up, and finally bedtime cuddles. Record your pet's voice — you be the voice actor!

📄 Pages (5 scenes) 🎤 Voice acting ⏳ Timing ♾️ Sleep loop 🟣 Show/Hide
Quick start steps
1
Plan on paper: 5 scenes — morning, park, lunch, nap, bedtime. Draw a storyboard!
2
Record your pet's narration for each scene in your best animal voice
3
Sleep scene: zzz characters appear in a forever loop and pet slowly shrinks
