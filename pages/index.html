👨‍👩‍👧 Who It's For

Built for Real Families,
Not Classrooms

Designed around how kids actually learn at home — through play, drawing, and projects they're genuinely proud of.

👩‍💻
Parents with zero coding experience

Every session has a word-for-word script and guiding questions. You never need to know the answer — you just need to ask the right question and let them figure it out.

🧒
Kids aged 7–10

ScratchJr uses visual block — no typing, no syntax errors, no frustration. Kids learn real programming concepts like loops, events, and sequences through stories, dances, and games.

🏠
Home learners & homeschoolers

One focused hour a day, five days a week. Each lesson is self-contained and runs on a free iPad or Android app. No computer, no subscription, no special equipment needed.

🎓
After-school club teachers

Printable cards let kids work semi-independently at their own pace while you move around. Scripts make it easy to delegate to a co-teacher or classroom assistant.

⚡ Getting Started

Up and Running
in Under 10 Minutes

No setup, no accounts, no installs — except the free ScratchJr app.

1
Download ScratchJr

Free on the App Store (iPad) and Google Play (Android). No account — just open and go.

2
Print Week 1 Cards

Open Activity Cards, print Monday–Friday of Week 1. Place one card next to the tablet each morning.

3
Open Your Lesson Script

Keep Lesson Scripts open on your phone. Follow the timing, ask the questions, and celebrate every small win!
