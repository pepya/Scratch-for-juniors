📅 6 Weeks · 30 Lessons

The Full Curriculum

Day-by-day breakdown of every lesson, activity, and Friday mini-project across 6 weeks

Week 1 🐱 Week 2 🦁 Week 3 🔁 Week 4 📨 Week 5 🎵 Week 6 🏆
01
Hello, ScratchJr! 🐱
Discover the app, meet the characters, and make your first animation
Motion Blocks Triggers Looks Blocks Drawing
🐱
Day 1 · Monday
Meet the Playground 🛝
  • Open ScratchJr — explore every button together
  • Name the Stage, Blocks panel, and Characters tray
  • Tap a character — watch it respond!
  • Draw your own character in the paint tool
⭐ Challenge: Name your character and save your first project
Day 2 · Tuesday
Move It! Arrow Blocks 🏃
  • Discover blue Motion blocks (left, right, up, down)
  • Drag and chain movement blocks together
  • Change the speed numbers and observe the difference
  • Build a dance by chaining 5+ moves
⭐ Challenge: A 5-block dance routine!
Day 3 · Wednesday
Press Play! Start Blocks ▶️
  • Find yellow Trigger blocks (green flag, tap character)
  • Connect a trigger to the start of a script
  • Press the green flag — see the difference!
  • Try all trigger types — what does each one do?
⭐ Challenge: Make it start both ways — flag AND tap!
Day 4 · Thursday
Look Different! Looks Blocks 🎨
  • Find purple Looks blocks
  • Make the character grow big and shrink small
  • Add a speech bubble with "Hello!"
  • Try hide and show — now you see it, now you don't!
⭐ Challenge: Grow → say hello → shrink → hide! One script!
Day 5 · Friday 🎉
Mini-Project: Hello World!
  • Choose a background scene
  • Add 2 characters (draw at least one)
  • Both characters move and say something
  • Present to your parent — explain every block
⭐ Challenge: Record a video of your project playing!
Warm-up 5 min Learn new block 20 min Free explore 20 min Challenge 10 min Show & tell 5 min
02
Tell a Story! 📖
Build multi-scene stories with dialogue, timing, and page navigation
PagesDialogueWait BlocksBackground Art
🦁
Day 1 · Monday
Scene 1 & Scene 2 🎭
  • Add a second page — different background!
  • Learn the "go to page" orange block
  • Connect pages: end of page 1 → jump to page 2
  • Test the page jump — does it work?
⭐ Challenge: 3-page story with automatic page jumps!
Day 2 · Tuesday
Characters Talking 💬
  • Act out the conversation out loud first
  • Use "say" blocks for each character
  • Add Wait blocks so they take turns
  • Adjust timing until it feels natural
⭐ Challenge: A 4-line back-and-forth conversation!
Day 3 · Wednesday
Wait For Me! Timing ⏳
  • Discover the orange Wait block
  • Build a sunrise: character → sun → birds, each timed
  • Try wait=1 vs wait=4 — feel the difference
  • Ask: does speed change the mood of a scene?
⭐ Challenge: A 5-event timed scene, one thing after another!
Day 4 · Thursday
Draw Your World 🖌️
  • Open the background paint editor
  • Draw: sky first, ground, then details
  • Draw a custom character to match the world
  • Add both to the project and make it move
⭐ Challenge: Original background + matching character!
Day 5 · Friday 🎉
Mini-Project: My First Story!
  • Write a 3-sentence plan on paper first
  • Build 3 pages with movement and dialogue
  • Add a title page with your name as author
  • Narrate aloud while it plays!
⭐ Challenge: You're a real author now — sign your story! ✍️
Review 5 min New skill 20 min Build scene 25 min Share 10 min
03
Loops & Repeats! 🔁
Unlock the secret of loops — patterns, dances, and continuous animations
Repeat LoopForever LoopPatternsDance
🔁
Day 1 · Monday
Why Repeat? The Loop Idea 🤔
  • Physical: clap 5 times — that's a loop!
  • Find the orange Repeat block
  • Build loop version vs 5 separate blocks
  • Compare — which was easier to build?
⭐ Challenge: Make a loop that runs 10 times. How far does it go?
Day 2 · Tuesday
Forever Loop! 🌀
  • Discover the Forever loop — it never stops!
  • Make a bird flap its wings forever (grow+shrink)
  • Make the sun spin forever with a turn block
  • Stop with the red button — learn to control it
⭐ Challenge: 2 characters, different forever loops — both at once!
Day 3 · Wednesday
Dance Party! 🕺💃
  • Design a 4-move dance on paper first
  • Put 4 moves inside a Repeat 8 loop
  • Add a second dancer with a different dance
  • Make one big, one small — more dynamic!
⭐ Challenge: Add a third dancer — 3 different dances at once!
Day 4 · Thursday
Patterns & Art 🌺
  • Use move + turn inside a loop = pattern path!
  • Experiment with different turn amounts
  • Add more characters with different patterns
  • Create a repeating pattern you love
⭐ Challenge: Design a pattern cool enough for a T-shirt! 👕
Day 5 · Friday 🎉
Mini-Project: Dance Show!
  • Draw a colourful stage background
  • 3+ characters each with a unique loop dance
  • Title page: "Welcome to the Show!"
  • Perform it for the family — be the announcer!
⭐ Challenge: Dance alongside your characters while it plays! 🕺
Concept intro 10 min Guided build 25 min Free create 20 min Reflect 5 min
04
Characters Talking to Each Other! 📨
Learn messages — make characters react to each other like magic
Send MessageReceive MessageEventsSimple Games
📨
Day 1 · Monday
What's a Message? 📬
  • Real life: shout "GO!" → friend starts running
  • Find Send and Receive message blocks
  • Character A sends → Character B reacts!
  • Key rule: sender and receiver must be same colour
⭐ Challenge: A sends message → B grows huge, then B sends back → A spins!
Day 2 · Tuesday
Chain Reactions ⛓️
  • Build a domino chain: A → B → C
  • Each character reacts AND sends the next message
  • Use different colour messages for each link
  • Press play — watch the chain! 😱
⭐ Challenge: Extend to a 4-character domino chain!
Day 3 · Wednesday
Tap & Respond 👆
  • Use "when character is tapped" trigger
  • Tap dog → barks + sends message → cat runs away!
  • Add speech bubbles for funny reactions
  • Build a simple tap-interaction mini-scene
⭐ Challenge: Tap one character — all THREE react differently!
Day 4 · Thursday
Build a Simple Game 🎮
  • "Catch the Star" — star bounces in a forever loop
  • Tap star → sends WIN message → star hides
  • Add a "YAY!" character that appears on WIN
  • Play 5 times, then make it harder!
⭐ Challenge: You built a real game! Tell someone how it works!
Day 5 · Friday 🎉
Mini-Project: Interactive Story!
  • Page 1: character at two doors — left and right
  • Tap left door → happy ending (Page 2)
  • Tap right door → funny ending (Page 3)
  • Let a parent play — can they find both endings?
⭐ Challenge: You built a choose-your-own-adventure app! 🚀
Concept + demo 15 min Build together 20 min Solo create 20 min Present 5 min
05
Sound & Music! 🎵
Add sound effects, record your voice, and build musical animations
Sound BlocksVoice RecordingInstrumentsSync
🎵
Day 1 · Monday
Make Some Noise! 🔊
  • Find the green Sound blocks
  • Add a pop sound when character is tapped
  • Try different sounds: boing, meow, bell
  • Trigger sounds at start, on tap, after a move
⭐ Challenge: 3 characters, 3 different sounds — tap each one!
Day 2 · Tuesday
Record Your Voice! 🎤
  • Tap the microphone icon in ScratchJr
  • Record "Hello! I am [name]!" loud and clear
  • Attach to a character — tap it and hear yourself!
  • Try funny voices: robot, whisper, monster 👾
⭐ Challenge: Record a narration for your Week 2 story! 🎬
Day 3 · Wednesday
Musical Instruments 🎹
  • Draw 4 colourful characters = 4 notes
  • Each gets a different sound on tap
  • Try playing do-re-mi-fa on your instrument!
  • Practice Happy Birthday 🎂
⭐ Challenge: Play a tune — can your parent guess the song?
Day 4 · Thursday
Sync Sound & Motion 🎬
  • Make a character jump + play sound simultaneously
  • Sound before vs after a move — spot the difference!
  • Build fireworks: burst animation + pop sound
  • Record "3… 2… 1… Launch!" as voice countdown
⭐ Challenge: A 5-second sound + motion show — like a movie trailer!
Day 5 · Friday 🎉
Mini-Project: Music Video!
  • Pick a theme: jungle, space, or underwater
  • 3+ animated characters moving in loops
  • Record an original song or poem
  • Perform it live for the family! 🎤🌟
⭐ Challenge: Sing your song live while the video plays!
Sound exploration 10 min Build project 30 min Perform & share 20 min
06
Grand Finale! 🏆
Apply everything — design, build, polish and present your own original project
Independent BuildCreative DesignPresentationGraduation
🏆
Day 1 · Monday
Big Idea Planning Day 💡
  • Review all 5 weeks — what was your favourite?
  • Brainstorm: story, game, music video, or something new?
  • Draw the plan on paper: characters, scenes, actions
  • Tell your parent your idea — they're your first audience!
⭐ Challenge: Write 3 things your project MUST include — your promise!
Day 2 · Tuesday
Build: Art Day 🎨
  • Draw ALL characters in the paint tool
  • Create all backgrounds for every scene
  • Set up all pages in the right order
  • No code yet — just art! Make it beautiful first
⭐ Challenge: All art assets ready — take a screenshot of your work!
Day 3 · Wednesday
Build: Add the Code 🧩
  • Add motion, loops, messages to each character
  • Test each script as you build — don't wait!
  • Fix bugs: "why did that happen?" debug together
  • Add sound and voice recordings
⭐ Challenge: Play through the whole project — write 3 things to improve!
Day 4 · Thursday
Polish & Improve ✨
  • Play through — what's missing? What needs fixing?
  • Add title screen with your name as creator!
  • Add "The End" or "Play Again" on last page
  • Let parent play without help — watch what confuses them
⭐ Challenge: Project 100% done — save one final time! 💾
Day 5 · Friday 🎓
Graduation Day! 🏆
  • Present to the whole family 👨‍👩‍👧
  • Explain what each part does and how you built it
  • "What was hardest? What are you most proud of?"
  • Celebrate with your favourite treat! 🍰🌟
⭐ You are now officially a ScratchJr Coder! What's next? 🚀
Free build 45 min Parent check-in 10 min Wrap-up 5 min
